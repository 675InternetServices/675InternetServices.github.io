Our projects

Divehub makes planning your next dive trip easy. Through our database of dive sites and diving centers we help scuba divers around the world to plan their next dive whether it's a short one day trip or a three week vacation. Reviews from fellow divers help in choosing the best destination.

Using Divehub divers can keep track of their dives in their logbook. With Divehub they will never loose their logbook again. Keeping track of dives in their Divehub logbook gives fellow divers insight in diving conditions at the dive sites visited.

Urban Farm offers Dutch and Belgian consumers everything they need to start their own urban farm. Growing fruits, herbs and vegatables at home is not diffucult and you don't need a large garden to do so. With the products we offer we want to enable everyone to grow their own food. We keep a blog of our own efforts and post regularly on Instagram.

Urban Farm offers a large range of products for sharp prices, quick delivery times at low costs with a 30 day return policy.
